The Cubism Art Museum was founded in 2010 and opened its doors in 2011. It was the first museum to cover all fields of cubism, open to visitors from across the world.

Enlightenment ideals and values – critical scrutiny of all assumptions, open debate, scientific research, progress and tolerance – have marked the Museum since its foundation.

The Museum is driven by an insatiable curiosity for the world, a deep belief in objects as reliable witnesses and documents of cubism history, sound research, as well as the desire to expand and share knowledge.
